About Us

VB Africa + is a network of highly skilled young leaders from diverse industries, who provide young people, mostly in the university, with career guidance, internship application support and other skill set to prepare themselves to be highly competitive on international standards.

WHAT WE OFFER

Mentoring and coaching to help young people have a fair idea about their career goals
Empower young Africans to be confident and be internationally competitive
Provide them with available opportunities along their career paths
Provide guidance and help through application processes: CV, cover letters and interview preps
